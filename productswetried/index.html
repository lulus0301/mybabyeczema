Products We Tried

I should have taken a picture of all the products we had. It is difficult to think back to the chaos and recall all the products we tried. Here is the list of some of the products that I remember putting on Baby J at the beginning months of TSW. After months of trial and still seeing redness and ooze, we decided to try moisture withdrawal. One morning Baby J woke up with no redness on his skin after a night of wet wrap. I proceeded to do our usual skincare routine and applied moisturizer all over. Almost instantly, the redness came back. It looked like he was wearing red sleeves and leggings. Perhaps it was the product, but we have applied so much on his skin that maybe his skin needed a break from it all. We decided to allow his skin to heal naturally on the outside, while we focused on diet to heal from within. Moisture withdrawal worked for Baby J after all.
