My Why...

During the height of our topical steroid withdrawal (TSW) journey, I was desperate to find other TSW families who knew exactly what we were going through, so we wouldn't feel so alone. It was hard. I am not on any social media, although I briefly joined Facebook and Instagram. To date, I have only met a handful of TSW families, all of whom live in other states.

I decided to create an invite-only community of my own, Allergy Fam. This is not on Facebook or other traditional social media platforms. Allergy Fam is on Discord, where they do not sell your data or share it with third parties for advertising purposes. Your privacy and identity are yours.

This community focuses on healthy living and allergies, food and environmental. However, there is a special channel on eczema and TSW. Eczema is not completely behind us, but food allergies are our current battle. I would love to connect with other families who understand the struggles, or families who simply want to learn more about eating whole foods, allergies and skin issues.

I am passionate about eating whole foods and living a natural, holistic lifestyle. On Allergy Fam, we will support and empower one another, share stories, tips, pertinent research articles, resources, recommendations, recipes, and more.
